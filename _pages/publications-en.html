Research

Selected journal articles and conference papers. Each item links to a brief research summary with full-text and PDF links.

Journal Articles

2025 LI W, Li S, Du H, Huang Q, Zhuo Y, Zhou L, et al. Improving multibreed genomic prediction for breeds with small populations by modeling heterogeneous genetic (co)variance blockwise accounting for linkage disequilibrium. Journal of Animal Science and Biotechnology. 2025;16(1):176.

Models heterogeneous genetic covariance by LD blocks to improve multibreed genomic prediction, especially for breeds with small population sizes.

 2024 Li W, Zhang M, Du H, Wu J, Zhou L, Liu J. Multi-Trait Bayesian Models Enhance the Accuracy of Genomic Prediction in Multi-Breed Reference Populations. Agriculture. 2024;14(4):626.

Uses multi-trait Bayesian models to borrow information across breeds and improve genomic prediction in multibreed reference populations.

 2022 Li W, Wang Z, Luo S, Wu J, Zhou L, Liu J. Genome-Wide Association Analysis and Genetic Parameters for Feed Efficiency and Related Traits in Yorkshire and Duroc Pigs. Animals. 2022;12(15):1902.

Estimates genetic parameters and identifies SNPs and candidate genes related to feed efficiency traits in Yorkshire and Duroc pigs.

Conference Papers

2023 Li W, Li S, Liu J. Bayesian models for multibreed genomic selection considering covariance heterogeneity. The 22nd National Symposium on Animal Genetics and Breeding, Shanxi, 2023.

Presented a Bayesian multibreed genomic selection model that accounts for heterogeneous covariance across chromosome blocks.
